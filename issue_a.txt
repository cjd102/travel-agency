FEATURE REQUEST: Flight Reviews & Rating System

FEATURE TITLE:
Implement Flight Reviews and Ratings for User Feedback and Social Proof

FEATURE DESCRIPTION:
Currently, users have no way to share feedback about their flight experiences
or see what other travelers think about specific flights. This feature will 
enable users to leave star ratings (1-5 stars) and written reviews on flights
they have booked, helping other users make informed booking decisions.

The reviews system will display all reviews for each flight, allowing the
community to collectively identify the best travel options based on real
user experiences.
